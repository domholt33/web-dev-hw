Cannoli

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Marsala wine
  • Egg
  • Vegatable oil
  • Ricotta
  • Powdered sugar
  • Mini chocolate chips
  • Cinnamon
  • Pistachios(optional)

Directions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a food processor.
  2. Add marsala wine and egg and pulse to bring together.
  3. Heat oil in pot.
  4. Roll dough out very thin on a floured surface.
  5. Cut into rounds or ovals (about 4-inches each).
  6. Wrap rounds around greased cannoli forms, brush top or bottom edge with egg white to seal shut.
  7. Remove cannoli shells and drain on paper towels.
  8. Remove shells from forms, let forms cool and repeat process.
  9. Make cannoli filling.
  10. Let shells cool completely then fill with cannoli filling.
